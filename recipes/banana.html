Hot Banana Water

Description

This was my grandmother's recipe. She made us drink it. Many fond memories. Serve warm in mugs with whipped cream and marshmalloows for an extra treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 quart water
  • 2 banana peels

Steps

  1. Cut off the ends of the banana peels
  2. Boil water in a large pot
  3. Add 1 banana peel to boiling water
  4. Turn off heat, cover, and wait for 10 minutes
  5. Add 1 more banana peels and wait for an additional 10 minutes
  6. Strain into mugs and serve warm
